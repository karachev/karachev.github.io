Privacy Policy for Just Portfolio

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Overview

Just Portfolio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

All your portfolio data is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services to display stock prices:

  • Finnhub API: Used to fetch current stock prices and company information for US stocks.
  • Yahoo Finance: Used as a fallback for stocks on European exchanges (London, Germany, Amsterdam).

These services may collect anonymous usage data according to their own privacy policies. The app is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Data Storage

All portfolio data (transactions, holdings, settings) is stored exclusively on your device using:

  • iOS local storage (UserDefaults, SwiftData)
  • iOS Keychain for secure data storage

Your data is never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
