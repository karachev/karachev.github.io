Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new transaction?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter a ticker symbol, purchase date, number of shares, and price per share. For US stocks, use the standard symbol (e.g., AAPL). For European stocks, you can enter just the symbol (e.g., CSPX) and the app will automatically search European exchanges.

How do I enter European stock symbols?

You can enter European stocks in two ways: either enter just the symbol (e.g., CSPX) and the app will automatically search London, German, and Amsterdam exchanges, or specify the exchange suffix explicitly (e.g., CSPX.L for London, CSPX.DE for Germany, CSPX.AS for Amsterdam).

Where is my data stored?

All your portfolio data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server. Your data is private and secure.

How do I set my tax residence?

Go to Settings and select your country under "Tax Residence". This affects the holding period calculation for tax-free status.

Why doesn't the current price show for my stock?

The app uses Finnhub API for US stocks and Yahoo Finance for European exchanges. If a price doesn't appear, the ticker may be delisted or misspelled. The app automatically tries multiple exchanges for European stocks, but you can also specify the exchange explicitly (e.g., CSPX.L for London Stock Exchange).

Can I export my portfolio data?

Currently, data export is not available in this version. This feature may be added in future updates.

How do I delete a transaction?

Swipe left on any transaction in the list and tap the delete button.

Supported Stock Exchanges

The app supports stocks and ETFs from the following exchanges:

  • US Markets: NYSE, NASDAQ (e.g., AAPL, MSFT, TSLA)
  • London Stock Exchange: Use .L suffix (e.g., CSPX.L, VWRL.L) or enter symbol without suffix
  • German Exchanges: Use .DE suffix (e.g., CSPX.DE, VWCE.DE) or enter symbol without suffix
  • Amsterdam Exchange: Use .AS suffix (e.g., CSPX.AS) or enter symbol without suffix

The app automatically searches multiple exchanges when you enter a symbol without a suffix.
