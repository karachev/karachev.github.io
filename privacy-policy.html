Privacy Policy for Guess the Flag

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Guess the Flag ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Guess the Flag is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal, non-personal information to understand how the app is used and to improve your experience.

Analytics Data

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data. This helps us understand:

  • How users interact with different game modes and features
  • Which difficulty levels are most popular
  • App stability and performance metrics
  • General user engagement patterns

What we collect: Game mode usage, difficulty level selections, session duration, quiz completion rates, and feature interactions. This data is completely anonymous and aggregated.

Data Retention: Firebase Analytics data is automatically deleted after 60 months (5 years) of inactivity.

Your Control: You can disable analytics collection at any time through the app's Settings menu. When you disable analytics, no new data is sent to Firebase, though previously collected data cannot be deleted.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game Progress: Your best scores for each difficulty level
  • Preferences: Your selected language preference and analytics consent choice

All data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults and remains on your device. This data is only accessible by the app and is deleted if you uninstall the app.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA is not used for tracking purposes)
  • Crash reports
  • Any personal or sensitive information

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase Analytics: For anonymous, aggregated usage analytics. Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Terms of Service. No personal information is shared with Firebase.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect data to serve personalized ads based on your usage patterns and interests. For more information, visit Google's Advertising Policies.

Advertisements and Ad Personalization

Our app displays advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your app usage and browsing behavior within the app to provide more relevant advertising.

We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage user consent for:

  • Data collection for ad personalization
  • Analytics data collection
  • Compliance with privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, and other regional privacy laws)

Your Control: You can manage your consent preferences at any time by going to Settings > Privacy & Consent in the app. You can also opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

GDPR and Your Privacy Rights

If you are in the European Union or any region with GDPR protections, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request to know what data we collect about you
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for data collection at any time through Settings > Privacy & Consent
  • Right to Object: You can object to ad personalization and analytics collection

To exercise any of these rights or for privacy-related questions, please contact us at karachevpavel@icloud.com.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there is no risk of your information being intercepted or accessed by unauthorized parties through our app.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it:

  • You can reset your game progress at any time by reinstalling the app
  • You can change your language preference in the app settings
  • All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Consent

By using Guess the Flag, you consent to this Privacy Policy. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data processing that requires your additional consent.
