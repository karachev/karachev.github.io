About the App

Guess the Flag is an educational quiz game that helps you learn and memorize flags from all 193 UN-recognized countries. Whether you're a student, traveler, or geography enthusiast, our app makes learning flags engaging and fun!

Features

  • 5 difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, Hard, Extreme, and Challenge Mode
  • All 193 UN-recognized countries
  • 9 languages: English, Russian, French, Italian, German, Spanish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean
  • Track your best scores for each difficulty
  • Challenge Mode with 5 lives for ultimate test
  • Clean, ad-free interface
  • Works offline - no internet required

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the language?
Tap the Settings icon (gear) on the home screen, then select your preferred language from the list. The app interface and country names will update immediately.
How does the Challenge Mode work?
Challenge Mode tests you on all 193 countries with 5 lives. Each wrong answer costs one life. The game ends when you run out of lives or complete all countries.
What's the difference between difficulty levels?
• Easy: 20 popular countries (USA, UK, France, etc.)
• Medium: 50 well-known countries
• Hard: 83 less-known countries
• Extreme: 40 tiny island nations
• Challenge: All 193 countries with limited lives
How are best scores saved?
Your best score for each difficulty level is automatically saved on your device. The app compares your score after each quiz and updates the best score if you improve.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! All content is built into the app, so you can practice learning flags anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.
Does the app collect my data?
No. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your scores and language preference are stored only on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Can I reset my progress?
Your best scores are saved permanently. To reset all data, you can delete and reinstall the app. Each quiz session can be restarted by tapping "Start Again" after completion.
