FAQ 💬

How do I connect with my partner? ⌄
Open the Partner tab and tap Create Session. Share the 6-character code with your partner. They tap Join Session, enter the code, and you're instantly connected. Matches appear in real time as you both swipe.
Can I use Baby Names without a partner? ⌄
Yes! You can swipe and build a personal list of names you like without connecting to anyone. Matches only appear when both users in a session like the same name, but the Likes list works fully on its own.
How do I undo a swipe? ⌄
Tap the undo button (curved arrow icon) at the bottom of the swipe screen to go back one name.
What languages are the names available in? ⌄
Baby Names includes names in English, Russian, Ukrainian, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Swedish, Danish, Turkish, Arabic, Hindi, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Malay, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and more. New languages are added regularly.
