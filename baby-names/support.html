Frequently asked questions

How do I connect with my partner?

Open the Partner tab and tap Create Session. Share the 6-character code with your partner. They tap Join Session, enter the code, and you're connected. Matches will appear in real time.

Why am I not seeing new names?

You may have reviewed all names for your current filter settings. Try changing the gender, name language, or removing the starting letter or length filter. You can also reset all your swipes from the Filters screen.

How do I undo a swipe?

Tap the undo button (curved arrow) at the bottom of the swipe screen to go back one name.

How do I reset all my swipes?

Go to Filters and tap Reset All Swipes. This lets you start fresh with all names.

Can I use the app without a partner?

Yes. You can swipe and save liked names without connecting to anyone. Matches only appear when both users in a session like the same name, but the Likes list works independently.

Does the app require an account or login?

No. Baby Names works without any sign-up. A random anonymous ID is created automatically on first launch — no email or password required.

How do I delete my data?

Email us at karachevpavel@icloud.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will remove all server-side data associated with your anonymous ID within 7 days.
